Absurd Running is a planning tool for ultra runners. You build a race plan on the web — pacing, fueling, gear, drop bags, and alerts — and the mobile app runs it offline on race day. No signal needed, no battery panic.

These docs cover everything from uploading your first route to syncing your plan to your watch.

The fastest way to get a specific question answered is the **Help** button (the `?` icon) in the app sidebar. It knows what page you're on and shows relevant help inline.

For anything these docs don't cover, email [hello@absurdrunning.com](mailto:hello@absurdrunning.com) — a real person responds.
